## **Overview**

I recently attended an insightful industry talk by **PPG**, a global leader in paints, coatings, and specialty materials. The session delved into their IT strategy, data analytics capabilities, infrastructure models, and SAP integration. The speaker also shared valuable career insights, highlighted innovation pathways, and introduced PPG's **MRTC Internship Program**.

PPG operates in **70+ countries** with over **140 years** of industry experience. Their vision is to become the world's leading coatings company through innovative and sustainable solutions.

Thank you to the speaker for sharing real-world IT experiences and emphasizing the importance of continuous learning and adaptability in the tech industry.

## **Key Takeaways from the Session**

### 1. **IT Infrastructure & Multi-Cloud Strategy**
- PPG uses a **multi-cloud approach** with **AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Oracle Cloud**
- Each cloud serves a specific purpose:
  - **AWS** for cloud-native applications
  - **Azure** for data lakes and virtual desktops
  - **Oracle Cloud** for licensed apps and databases
- Emphasis on **Zero Trust Security** to combat advanced cyber threats

### 2. **SAP Process Flow in Operations**
- The SAP system streamlines operations from **procurement to payment**
- Key steps include:
  1. Purchase order creation
  2. Supplier engagement
  3. Production
  4. Delivery
  5. Invoice and payment processing
- Ensures **efficiency, accuracy, and scalability** in global operations

### 3. **Data Analytics & Centers of Excellence (COEs)**
- Introduction to **Data Analytics COE, Global Infrastructure COE, and SAP COE**
- These centers drive innovation, standardization, and best practices across PPG
- Real-time data analytics supports decision-making and operational efficiency

### 4. **Career Pathways & MRTC Internship**
- PPG’s **MRTC Internship Program** offers hands-on experience in IT, data, cloud, and SAP
- The speaker highlighted the importance of skills in **Python, MySQL, Cloud Engineering, and SAP Analytics**
- Opportunities for students to engage with **real IT experts** and work on **global projects**
